About Green Beans Coffee

Tucked quietly into the Dagayasando neighborhood of Kitasando / Sendagaya, Shibuya, Green Beans Coffee is a specialty coffee shop known only to those in the know. The more I visited, the more I realized this is no ordinary café. Pets are welcome to relax inside, gluten-free options are on the menu, and hands-on experiences like hand-drip and roasting events make every visit something special. This shop has no storefront sign and no official website. This site is run by a GBC Ambassador to share the charm of Green Beans Coffee. We hope you'll come and experience it for yourself.

Menu

Hand-drip coffee brewed from 30+ house-roasted beans, freshly made gluten-free food, and house-made sweets that pair beautifully with coffee — every item on the Green Beans Coffee menu is crafted with care and warmth.

Coffee

Each cup is brewed by hand with care, served in a distinctive asymmetric cup with different rim thicknesses on each side. The left and right sides offer subtly different aromas and flavors — a unique experience you won't find anywhere else.

Fruity / Mild / Bitter / Decaf

Food

Don't miss the gluten-free one-plate lunch. It features house-made rice flour focaccia (baked fresh every morning) and a salad, plus your choice of main and side dish. Stay after lunch — drinks are half price until 3 PM.

Sweets

A moist rice flour chocolate brownie (gluten-free), a colorful house-made sweets plate, apple pie with caramel ice cream, and creamy ice cream made with premium Hokkaido milk — all crafted to complement the coffee.
